Educrypto : Transforming Education through Blockchain

EduCrypto is an Ethereum blockchain and IPFS based system for storing, retrieving, and authenticating educational documents as well as streamlining the entire process of examination and result generation.
The system aims to automate the generation of grade sheets and mark sheets for the faculty and the exam section respectively.

The system comprises three main modules: Admission; Examination and Results; Third-Party Module.
